LightZone editions

LightZone comes in two editions: "LightZone" and "LightZone Basic."

LightZone
LightZone is an all-in-one solution for your digital photography workflow. In addition to powerful editing tools, LightZone includes an integrated Photo Browser. In addition to displaying your photos, the integrated browser enables you to copy & paste the tool stack from one photo to others and also perform batch converting of photos.
LightZone Basic
LightZone Basic has all the same powerful editing tools as LightZone, but costs less and so has fewer features. However, LightZone Basic makes a great "external editor" for many other photo organization applications including Aperture, iPhoto, iView MediaPro, or Lightroom.
