ホワイトバランスの調整

The White Balance tool allows you to adjust the white balance and tint of your photo to compensate for a color cast in the light source such as the blue cast of the sky or tungsten lighting.

Note: For raw photos, you should use the Temperature and Tint sliders of the Raw Adjustments tool instead.

ホワイトバランスを調整するには:

  1. Click the white balance tool button in the tool bar. (A new white balance tool will appear at the top of the tool stack.)
  2. 以下のどれかを行います。
    1. Use the Temperature and Tint sliders to adjust the white balance and tint.
    2. Click the eyedropper button inside the tool (the mouse will change to an eyedropper), then click somewhere on your photo that you want to be neutral gray. (Your photo's colors will then shift accordingly.) When satisfied with the color cast reduction, click the eyedropper button again to complete the adjustment.
  3. 追加事項:
    • 領域を使ってマスクを作成します。
    • 別のブレンド モードを選択します。
    • 不透明度スライダーを調整します。
    • 色/輝度を選択します。
