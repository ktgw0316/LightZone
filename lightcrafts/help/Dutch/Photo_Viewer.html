Photo Viewer

In the center of the Browser window is Photo Viewer that shows one to five large previews of the selected thumbnails in the selected folder in the Folder Navigator.

To view a large preview of a photo:

  1. Select the thumbnail of the photo.

To view up to five a large previews of photos:

  1. Hold the Shift key to select multiple, contiguous thumbnails or the Command Control key to select multiple, non-contiguous thumbnails.

To edit one of the photos:

  1. Click anywhere in the photo's preview.
