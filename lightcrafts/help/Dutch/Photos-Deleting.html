Deleting photos

You can delete the image files of your photos directly in LightZone using the Photo Browser.

To delete photos:

  1. Select one or more thumbnails of the photos in the browser that you want to delete. (Gebruik de Shift toets om meerdere, achtereenvolgende miniaturen te selecteren of de Command Control toets om meerdere, niet aaneengesloten miniaturen te selecteren).
  2. Select Browser > Move to Trash... Recycle Bin...
  3. Click Move to Trash Recycle Bin (or press Return).
